What we do

Our services

01

Pensions & Retirement Planning

We advise pension fund trustees, sponsors, and individuals on strategy, compliance, and investment decisions that secure long-term retirement outcomes.

  • Pension fund governance & trustee advisory
  • Defined benefit & defined contribution scheme review
  • Retirement income strategy & decumulation planning
  • Regulatory compliance & reporting (NPRA, SSNIT)
  • Member communication & education programmes
  • Investment policy review & asset allocation
02

Corporate Governance

We support boards, executives, and shareholders in building governance frameworks that drive accountability, transparency, and sustainable value creation.

  • Board effectiveness reviews & evaluations
  • Corporate governance codes implementation
  • Board composition, diversity & succession planning
  • Risk management frameworks & internal controls
  • Shareholder engagement & ESG advisory
  • Policy development for listed & unlisted entities
03

Housing & Mortgage Financing Advisory

We advise financial institutions, developers, and government agencies on structuring accessible and sustainable housing finance solutions.

  • Mortgage product design & portfolio review
  • Affordable housing finance strategy
  • Institutional lending framework development
  • Housing policy advisory for public sector
  • Real estate investment structuring
  • Home ownership schemes & rent-to-own design
